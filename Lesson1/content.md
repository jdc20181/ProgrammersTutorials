#What is Content?

Content is :

 - Text
 
 - Images 
 
 - Files
 
 - Buttons/Inputs 
 
 Content is a big part of a website, well the entire part of a website. Putting the right content in the right spot is a great idea. 
 
 For example, don't put too many sub headings in the same paragraph.
